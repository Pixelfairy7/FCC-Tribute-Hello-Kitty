Hello Kitty

Iconic. Beloved. Popculture Phenomenom.

About

Say Hello to everyone's favorite Kawaii Kitty! Hello Kitty is a Japanese cartoon character produced by Sanrio, created by Yuko Shimizu in 1974. If you want to be formal, her real name is Kitty White. Hello Kitty is a perpetual 3rd grader who lives outside of London. hello Kitty is a pop culture phenomenom whose fan base ranges from adolescents to adults.

Fun Facts

Here are some fun facts about Hello Kitty! Hello Kitty is actually not a cat.She is a little girl. She doesn't have a specific language and she doesn't have a mouth. That's so Hello Kitty can be relateable to everyone, and her viewer can project the emotion of their choice onto her. Go ahead. Talk to her. Feels good doesn't it? She does happen to be British. And her birthday is November 1st. Kitty has been featured on a variety of products from cosmetics, appliances, luxury jewelry, and even Airbus A-330 airplanes!
